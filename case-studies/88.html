88 Collective – Driving Growth in Regulated E-Commerce (CBD)

A case study demonstrating how strategic, compliant marketing can thrive in heavily restricted e-commerce environments.

Context

88 Collective was a CBD e-commerce reseller in Ireland. Marketing CBD products is heavily restricted — Meta, Google, and other ad networks limit or block direct promotion. The challenge was to grow online sales under compliance constraints, navigating a complex regulatory landscape.

The Challenge

  • Paid Ad Restrictions: CBD promotions were restricted or blocked on major platforms.
  • Higher CPCs: Limited ad approval led to inflated Cost-Per-Click.
  • Low Consumer Trust: CBD required significant education to build confidence.
  • Fragmented Tracking: Compliance optimization necessitated complex and often disjointed tracking solutions.

Strategic Goals

  • Compliant Funnels: Build robust funnels to drive CBD product sales within regulations.
  • Lower CAC: Achieve a reduced Customer Acquisition Cost despite stringent ad restrictions.
  • Improve Trust: Enhance consumer confidence through education-based marketing funnels.

My Role

  • Campaign Design: Devised performance campaigns that successfully navigated advertising restrictions.
  • Content Funnels: Constructed content-driven funnels for lead capture, nurturing, and eventual purchase.
  • Tracking & Attribution: Implemented comprehensive tracking and attribution in a highly restricted environment.

Our Strategic Approach

1. Funnel Strategy

  • Top-of-Funnel (TOF): Executed educational campaigns (e.g., “What is CBD? Benefits & Safety”) on Meta and Google Display Networks.
  • Middle-of-Funnel (MOF): Captured emails via gated content, followed by automated email nurturing sequences.
  • Bottom-of-Funnel (BOF): Drove retargeted traffic to product landing pages meticulously optimized for compliance.

2. Messaging Strategy

  • Compliance First: Strictly avoided medical claims to prevent ad bans and maintain platform compliance.
  • Benefit-Oriented: Focused messaging on lifestyle and relaxation benefits of CBD.
  • Trust & Quality: Emphasized product quality, responsible sourcing, and safety certifications.

3. Tracking & Optimization

  • Compliant Attribution: Utilized GA4 and server-side tracking to ensure accurate and compliant data attribution.
  • A/B Testing: Developed and tested multiple landing page variants to optimize performance.
  • Holistic Metrics: Shifted focus to Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and Cost-Per-Lead (CPL) rather than just raw CAC due to advertising limitations.

Key Learnings

  • Content-First Wins: In restricted categories, content-first funnels consistently outperform direct advertising approaches.
  • Credibility Through Compliance: Compliance-focused messaging is crucial for building long-term brand credibility and consumer trust.
  • Email as ROI Driver: Email nurturing emerged as the single highest-ROI channel for CBD product sales.

Overall Impact

88 Collective successfully transformed into a trusted CBD e-commerce brand despite the stringent advertising limitations. By implementing compliant funnels and education-led campaigns, this case study unequivocally demonstrates that performance marketing can not only survive but thrive, even in the most restricted and challenging categories.
