Creative Automation Tool

Scaling Ad Creative Production

Context

Performance marketing success depends on continuous A/B testing of creatives. However, producing creative variations at scale was slow and expensive, often requiring external design resources.

To solve this bottleneck, I built a custom creative automation system that used GPT for copy and Canva/Adobe APIs for design automation.

Challenge

  • Manual creative production caused delays in campaign launches.
  • Agencies charged high fees for generating variations.
  • Limited ability to test multiple creative angles quickly.
  • This limited campaign optimization and kept CAC higher than necessary.

Goals

  • Reduce creative production time by at least 80%.
  • Enable rapid A/B testing of multiple angles.
  • Cut reliance on expensive design agencies.

My Role

  • Designed and implemented the end-to-end automation flow.
  • Integrated AI copy generation with design APIs.
  • Connected workflow to campaign platforms for seamless testing.

Approach

1. Automation Design

  • Used GPT to generate ad copy variations (emotional, rational, urgency-driven).
  • Built modular creative templates in Canva + Adobe.
  • Automated creative generation pipeline via Integromat (Make) and Google Sheets inputs.

2. Testing Workflow

  • Exported 50–100 creative variations per campaign in minutes.
  • Uploaded directly into ad platforms for structured A/B tests.
  • Monitored CTR, CPC, and CVR to identify top performers.

3. Iteration

  • Set up feedback loop: winning creatives → expanded variations; losing creatives → discarded quickly.

Tools & Platforms

GPT Playground (copy generation)Canva API & Adobe API (design automation)Google Sheets + Make (workflow automation)

Key Learnings

  • Automation accelerates testing but human oversight remains critical for quality.
  • Different messaging angles (emotional vs. functional) must be rotated for fatigue prevention.
  • Cost savings + speed gave campaigns a competitive edge over slower competitors.

Impact

The system became a core asset in my performance marketing stack, enabling me to scale campaigns faster and more efficiently than relying on manual creative workflows.
