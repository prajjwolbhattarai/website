NAO Co-Investment GmbH

Investor Acquisition at Scale

Context

NAO Co-Investment GmbH is a fintech platform that connects private investors with exclusive co-investment opportunities. My role was to drive investor acquisition using digital channels while ensuring compliance, efficiency, and measurable ROI.

The Challenge

  • Fintech advertising restrictions limited messaging flexibility.
  • Leads were expensive, and many early campaigns generated low-quality contacts.
  • Tracking was fragmented across multiple platforms, making it hard to attribute leads to investments.

The challenge was to create a performance marketing engine that balanced cost efficiency with high-quality investor leads.

Key Goals

  • Generate qualified investor demos across channels.
  • Reduce CAC while maintaining lead quality.
  • Build multi-touch attribution for investor pipeline visibility.

My Role

  • Managed €45K+ monthly budget across multiple ad platforms.
  • Designed the acquisition funnel and performance framework.
  • Implemented server-side tracking and reporting dashboards.

My Approach

Multi-Channel Acquisition

  • LinkedIn Ads: targeted senior professionals and HNWIs by title and industry.
  • Google Search Ads: captured high-intent searches for “private equity investing” and related queries.
  • Meta + Reddit Ads: widened awareness among finance communities.
  • Spotify Ads: used as experimental awareness channel for investors.

Attribution & Tracking

  • Implemented GA4 + GTM with server-side LinkedIn CAPI.
  • Integrated Customer.io with HubSpot CRM for funnel tracking.
  • Built custom dashboards showing MQL → SQL → Investor → AUM progression.

Optimization Strategies

  • Shifted budget weekly toward channels delivering highest investor qualification rates, not just lowest CPL.
  • A/B tested messaging angles: trust (whitepapers, case studies) vs. exclusivity (“access elite deals”).

Key Learnings

  • For fintech, trust-driven content (case studies, insights) converted far better than generic ads.
  • Multi-touch attribution avoided wasted spend on surface-level clicks.
  • Spotify proved strong for awareness but weak for conversions — budget reallocated.

Overall Impact

I established NAO’s scalable investor acquisition engine, balancing compliance with efficiency. Performance marketing directly fueled the company’s ability to expand its investor base and pipeline.
