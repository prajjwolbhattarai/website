Pocket Studio

Scaling a Restaurant SaaS with Performance Marketing

A deep dive into repositioning a product and building a robust performance marketing system to drive growth and enterprise adoption.

Context

Pocket Studio was a restaurant management SaaS that bundled POS, reservations, payroll, and inventory management. Despite its potential, the product was perceived as just another POS tool. Restaurants were using 5–7 fragmented SaaS products, resulting in inefficiencies and annual costs of €20K–40K.

I was brought in to reposition the product and build a performance marketing system that would lower CAC, increase demo-to-paid conversions, and prove ROI for restaurants — setting the foundation for enterprise adoption.

My Role

I led the end-to-end performance marketing strategy and execution:

  • Defined ICPs (independents vs. multi-location chains).
  • Designed full-funnel campaigns on Google, LinkedIn, and Meta.
  • Implemented attribution via GA4, HubSpot, and server-side tracking.
  • Built ROI dashboards to prove cost savings for restaurants.

The Challenge

Pocket Studio’s growth was stalling due to:

  • Weak perception: seen as a niche POS, not an all-in-one system.
  • High CPLs: but poor demo-to-paid conversion rates.
  • Fragmented tracking: no reliable CAC or ROAS data.
  • Competitive SaaS space: established tools with stronger brands.

The challenge was to reframe the product and design campaigns that could drive demo requests and conversions while reducing CAC.

Key Goals

  • Reduce CAC by at least 25%.
  • Increase demo-to-paid conversion rate.
  • Drive adoption in multi-location chains as well as independents.
  • Build a transparent attribution model across channels.

Our Strategic Approach

1. Audience Segmentation

  • Targeted independent restaurants via Meta (FB/Instagram).
  • Targeted enterprise F&B operators via LinkedIn (titles: Operations Manager, F&B Director).
  • Used Google Search Ads for high-intent queries.

2. Funnel Structure

  • TOF: Awareness via LinkedIn thought-leadership & Meta Ads.
  • MOF: Google Ads + retargeting to demo pages.
  • BOF: Demo requests nurtured via HubSpot, ROI calculators.

3. Attribution & Tracking

  • Implemented GA4 + GTM for cross-channel tracking.
  • Integrated HubSpot CRM for MQL→SQL→Customer flow.
  • Built custom UTM validator for consistency.

4. ROI Proof

  • Created Tableau dashboards showing per-client savings.
  • Used in ads (“Save €15K/year”) and as sales demo collateral.

Key Learnings

ROI-Driven Messaging

Outperformed feature-based ads by 70%, proving the power of value-centric communication.

Multi-Touch Funnel

Awareness → retargeting → demo nurture was critical in long B2B SaaS buying cycles, ensuring sustained engagement.

Attribution Dashboards

Enabled precise budget shifts toward high-quality leads, optimizing spend beyond just cheap CPLs.

Lasting Impact

Pocket Studio evolved from being seen as just another POS system into a credible all-in-one SaaS platform for restaurants. Performance marketing became the growth engine, fueling enterprise adoption and setting the foundation for acquisition.
