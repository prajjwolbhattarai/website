UG Cakes – Scaling E-Commerce Sales 16X

A Case Study: Driving Digital Transformation with Paid Performance

Context

UG Cakes, an e-commerce brand specializing in cakes and gifting, faced a significant challenge: offline orders dominated, while online channels remained underdeveloped. The brand lacked a structured sales funnel, a strong creative presence, and a robust attribution system for its digital efforts.

My objective was to design and execute a comprehensive performance marketing strategy to transform UG Cakes into a truly digital-first business.

Challenge

  • Heavy reliance on offline and referrals.
  • Online sales flat and unscalable.
  • High CAC with no retargeting or funnel optimization.
  • Lack of visibility on ROAS.

Goals

  • Scale online daily sales 10X within a year.
  • Build reliable attribution for marketing spend.
  • Reduce CAC while improving ROAS.

My Role

  • Built the entire performance funnel (Google + Meta).
  • Implemented tracking + attribution.
  • Designed creative campaigns tied to events and gifting occasions.

Approach

  1. 1.

    Funnel Build-Out

    A multi-stage funnel was designed to capture diverse user intent:

    • TOF (Top of Funnel): Facebook/Instagram ads showcasing cakes in lifestyle contexts to build brand awareness.
    • MOF (Middle of Funnel): Google Search + Shopping Ads targeting intent-driven buyers actively searching for cakes.
    • BOF (Bottom of Funnel): Retargeting ads with urgency messaging (“Order today, delivered today”) to convert warm leads.
  2. 2.

    Tracking & Attribution

    Robust tracking was essential for data-driven decisions:

    • Deployed Google Analytics + Meta Pixel for comprehensive data collection.
    • Monitored key metrics: CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), ROAS (Return on Ad Spend), AOV (Average Order Value).
    • Built weekly reports to dynamically shift budget allocation based on performance.
  3. 3.

    Creative & Offers

    Campaigns were tailored to resonate with specific customer needs:

    • Focused on event-driven marketing (birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gifting).
    • Utilized seasonal campaigns (Valentine’s, festivals) for timely engagement.
    • Introduced compelling bundle offers and same-day delivery to enhance value proposition.

Key Learnings

  • Event-driven messaging consistently outperformed generic promotions.
  • Strategic urgency combined with retargeting successfully closed the gap on impulse purchases.
  • Rigorous tracking and dynamic budget reallocation were critical for sustainable scaling and optimizing spend.

Impact

Through this initiative, UG Cakes successfully transitioned into a digital-first brand, with online sales not only catching up but ultimately overtaking offline sales. Performance marketing became the indisputable primary growth driver for the company, establishing a scalable and efficient path to market expansion.
