UG Cakes

Scaling E-Commerce Sales 16X

Context

UG Cakes (formerly Urban Girl) was an e-commerce company specializing in cakes and gifting. While the business had strong offline traction and word-of-mouth visibility, its online sales were limited. The brand was not leveraging digital channels effectively, and growth had plateaued.

"I was brought in to design and execute a digital growth engine with performance marketing at its core."

The Challenge

  • Sales heavily dependent on offline orders and referrals.
  • Weak digital funnel with limited paid activity.
  • No attribution model — marketing spend could not be tracked against revenue.
  • High CAC from early ad tests with little optimization.

The business needed a scalable performance marketing system that could transform online sales from a side channel into the main driver of growth.

Key Goals

  • Increase online daily sales by at least 10X.
  • Establish reliable attribution for ad spend.
  • Build retargeting loops to improve ROI.
  • Reduce CAC while scaling budgets.

My Role

I owned the end-to-end growth strategy, including:

  • Designing the full funnel (awareness → conversion → repeat purchase).
  • Launching campaigns across Meta Ads and Google Ads.
  • Building tracking infrastructure (GA + Pixel).
  • Iterating on creative, copy, and offers.

Our Approach

1. Customer Journey Audit

I began with an audit of customer behavior: most orders were driven by birthdays, celebrations, and gifting. This revealed an opportunity to focus campaigns on impulse gifting + event-based triggers.

2. Performance Marketing Execution

  • Meta Ads: Launched visual storytelling campaigns on Facebook and Instagram. Showcased cakes in lifestyle settings (birthdays, surprises, office parties).
  • Google Search + Shopping: Captured high-intent queries like “order birthday cake online” and “same-day cake delivery.”
  • Retargeting: Built custom audiences of cart abandoners and past buyers. Used urgency hooks like “Order today, delivered today.”

3. Attribution & Tracking

  • Implemented Google Analytics + Meta Pixel to track sales funnels.
  • Monitored CAC, ROAS, and AOV weekly.
  • Adjusted budgets dynamically to double down on best-performing campaigns.

Tools & Platforms

  • Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
  • Google Search + Shopping Ads
  • Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel

Tangible Results

MetricImpact
Average Daily SalesScaled by 16X within one year
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)Reduced by 40%
Retargeting Conversion Rate70% higher than prospecting
Primary Revenue ChannelOnline sales overtook offline

Key Learnings

  • Event-based messaging (birthdays, gifting) performed far better than generic promotions.
  • Retargeting + urgency (“same-day delivery”) consistently closed sales.
  • Tracking foundations allowed for data-driven scaling without wasted spend.

Overall Impact

UG Cakes was transformed into a scalable e-commerce brand, with online sales becoming the main revenue driver.

Performance marketing created a sustainable growth engine that outperformed offline channels.
