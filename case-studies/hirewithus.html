Hire With Us

Independent Product Launch Case Study

Context

Hire With Us was a new recruitment product designed to help employers streamline hiring. Unlike other company projects, I was tasked with leading this launch independently, while ensuring alignment with ongoing company initiatives.

Project Overview

  • New Recruitment Product
  • Designed for Employer Streamlining
  • Independent Launch Leadership
  • Alignment with Company Initiatives

The Challenge

  • Product had to launch quickly without affecting existing priorities.
  • Needed to generate early awareness and signups.
  • Marketing assets and funnels had to be built from scratch.

Key Goals

  • Deliver a smooth go-to-market launch.
  • Generate qualified signups for early adoption.
  • Maintain alignment with company roadmap.

My Role

Strategy & Execution

Designed and executed the launch strategy.

Asset Creation

Built the website, landing pages, and campaigns.

Management

Managed positioning and reporting independently.

My Approach

Positioning & Messaging

  • Framed Hire With Us as a cost-efficient recruitment solution.
  • Crafted messaging around speed, reliability, and employer value.

Launch Campaigns

  • Built demo-focused landing pages.
  • Ran multi-channel launch campaigns on Meta Ads and Google Ads.
  • Leveraged content + email marketing to drive early engagement.

Tracking & Reporting

  • Implemented GA4 for launch tracking.
  • Built weekly reporting dashboards to measure signups and funnel metrics.

Tools & Platforms Used

Meta AdsGoogle AdsGA4GTMHubSpot CRM

Key Results

  • Successfully launched the product with consistent user signups.
  • Established an initial pipeline of users for validation.
  • Achieved launch without disrupting other company initiatives.

The launch of Hire With Us was a resounding success, demonstrating effective strategy and execution under independent leadership.

Key Learnings

Independent launches require tight focus + lean execution.

Clear ROI messaging worked better than feature-heavy explanations.

Early signups provided strong product-market fit feedback.
