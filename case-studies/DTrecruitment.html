Recrunova – Marketing for Deep Tech Recruitment

Elevating Deep Tech Recruitment Through Targeted Marketing

Recrunova was a recruitment brand focused on sourcing deep tech talent across Europe. Traditional recruitment marketing was ineffective in reaching highly specialized profiles like senior developers, engineers, and researchers.

I built targeted recruitment marketing funnels to attract and engage deep tech professionals at scale, transforming how Recrunova connected with top-tier talent.

The Challenge

  • Deep tech talent is hard to reach via mainstream channels.
  • Generic job ads delivered low engagement and poor fit.
  • Needed credibility + precise targeting to reach niche audiences.

Key Goals

  • Increase awareness of Recrunova among deep tech talent.
  • Build a pipeline of qualified applications.
  • Lower CPL vs. traditional recruitment ads.

My Role & Contribution

  • Designed and executed marketing funnels for tech recruitment.
  • Automated creative production with AI tools.
  • Managed multi-channel targeting (LinkedIn, Reddit, niche communities).

Strategic Approach

Audience Targeting

Focused on senior engineers, developers, and researchers.

Used LinkedIn job titles, groups, and interests for precision.

Extended to Reddit sub-communities (e.g., r/MachineLearning, r/Programming).

Engagement Strategy

Created content that emphasized Recrunova’s credibility and unique opportunities.

Blended thought-leadership posts + targeted ads.

Creative Automation

Used GPT + Canva API to generate ad variations quickly.

Tested multiple messaging angles (career growth, innovation, compensation).

Tools & Platforms

  • LinkedIn Ads, Reddit Ads
  • GPT + Canva API
  • Google Sheets + Make

Tangible Results

✔

Qualified Pipeline

Delivered a steady pipeline of highly qualified tech candidates.

↓

Reduced CPL

CPL reduced significantly vs. generic recruitment campaigns.

↑

Brand Recognition

Increased brand recognition among niche deep tech communities.

Key Learnings

  • Deep tech audiences require community-driven engagement, not just ads.
  • Authentic messaging performed better than generic employer branding.
  • Creative automation improved testing speed and cut costs.

Overall Impact

Recrunova became known as a specialist recruitment platform for deep tech talent, achieving lower CPLs and higher quality applications through tailored marketing.

A testament to strategic, data-driven deep tech marketing.
