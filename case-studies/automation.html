Building Custom Growth & Revenue Tools to Improve Funnel Efficiency

An independent case study on solving common revenue team bottlenecks with bespoke automation.

Context

In my independent work, I’ve focused on solving the same problems revenue teams face every day:

  • •Poor CRM data hygiene.
  • •Limited visibility into pipeline health.
  • •Forecasts built on “gut feel” instead of structured data.
  • •Repetitive manual work slowing down growth teams.

I built a suite of lightweight, custom automation tools designed to fix these bottlenecks, cut wasted time, and make revenue processes repeatable, efficient, and predictable. These projects mirror Weflow’s mission — and proved how much impact can be achieved when sales and marketing teams get clean data, pipeline visibility, and workflow automation.

Challenge

The independent projects were designed to address 4 recurring issues I’d seen across SaaS, fintech, and e-commerce:

  • •Broken attribution: campaign UTMs weren’t standardized, making CAC/ROAS unreliable.
  • •Pipeline coverage fluctuated: no early warnings when SQLs stalled.
  • •Forecasting lacked structure: teams relied on gut instinct + messy sheets.
  • •Manual campaign operations: slowed down speed-to-market for testing and reporting.

Goals

  • •Build tools that ensure clean, consistent campaign data.
  • •Provide real-time pipeline visibility for sales + marketing.
  • •Automate repetitive workflows to free up growth teams.
  • •Create dashboards that could replace manual BI sheets with live reporting.

My Role

I acted as both architect and builder:

  • •Identified recurring problems through client interviews + my own growth work.
  • •Designed automation logic.
  • •Built lightweight tools using APIs, automation platforms, and analytics integrations.

Approach & Tools I Built

1. UTM Builder & Validator

  • Built a custom generator that standardized UTM parameters across campaigns.
  • Added a validation layer to auto-flag broken or duplicate links.

Result: Clean data flowed into analytics & CRM without manual checks.

2. Automated Funnel Dashboards

  • Connected GA4, HubSpot, and ad platforms into Tableau/Looker Studio.
  • Delivered real-time visibility from ad spend → MQL → SQL → revenue.
  • Added alerts when pipeline coverage dropped below healthy thresholds.

3. Creative Automation System

  • Built workflow combining GPT + Canva API to auto-generate ad copy + designs.
  • Produced 100+ creative variations in minutes instead of days.

Enabled faster A/B testing and cut design costs.

4. Workflow Automations (via Make)

  • Set up auto-syncs between campaign platforms and CRM.
  • Triggered alerts when deals stalled in CRM stages.
  • Automated weekly reporting → eliminating manual Excel/Sheets updates.

Results

Across projects, these tools delivered measurable improvements:

40%

improvement in tracking accuracy → reliable CAC/ROAS.

25%

lower CAC when clean attribution enabled smarter budget allocation.

80%

creative testing time cut → faster campaign velocity.

Forecasting confidence improved → pipeline dashboards gave early warnings.

Teams spent less time cleaning data and more time on selling and scaling.

Key Learnings

  • •CRM/pipeline data is only as good as the hygiene + workflows behind it.
  • •Automation doesn’t just save time — it unlocks scale (faster testing, better insights).
  • •Clean attribution transforms performance marketing into a predictable revenue machine.

Impact

Through these independent projects, I built my own version of a Revenue Operations toolkit: tools that auto-capture, attribute, and analyze data; surface deal risks; and drive better decision-making through automation.
